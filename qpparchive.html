Project Title: Springfield Beans Business Analysis

Group Members: Aanya Jain, Linda Fu, Vivek Patel, Srishti Sarkar

Project Description: Springfield Beans aims to eliminate their waste, specifically, when it comes to their supplies (disposable cups, lids, and straws). This excel file shows the cost analysis of what “eliminating waste” truly means for the company. First, we start off with Business outlook, which gives you a breakdown of our total revenue and what products are contributing the most to this waste. Next, we have Disposable Items Used: Analysis. This sheet explains different items used as well as the cost analysis for the drinkware used. In terms of the items used analysis, we look into the hot and cold drinks sold over the year, hot and cold drinks sold in relation to cup sizes, and hot and cold drinks sold in terms of seasonality and cup sizes. In terms of the cost analysis, we look at the annual cost of drinkware, costs based on both the locations, as well as cost based on hot or cold drink type. These charts will help analyze Springfield's drinkware consumption and costs, so they can move towards more sustainable and eco-friendly operations. This project showcases my excel skills, as well as my ability to provide business consultations based off of company data.

Project Title: SuperMarket Database Design & implementation

Group Members: Adam Warner, Thomas Xiong

Project Description: Our goal in this project was to create a database for Fresh Thyme that would assist the company in their day to day tasks. This database would allow Fresh Thyme to access key information about their members, suppliers, products and so much more in a quick and easy manner. In addition, this database has a number of constraints that are designed to minimize the instances of database errors. Ideally, any employee, experienced or inexperienced, would be able to access and understand the information coming from this database. Through the use of an ER diagram, a data dictionary, and logical schema, the inner workings of the database will be clearer to more technical employees. This will make it easier for them to change the database as they please in order to react to future situations. Towards the end of the project, we provide the SQL code that we used to implement the database. This project provided a real world application of my database overhaul skills, and provided a space for me to prove my SQL profeciency.
