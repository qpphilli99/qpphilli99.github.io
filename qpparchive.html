Project Title: How Stress Affects the Mind and Body (Health H170, Fall 2019)

Group Members: None

Project Description: The assignment for this project was to conduct research on how stress can negatively and positively affect the human mind and body. I began by gathering relevant information from multiple online resources. After noting common trends, I selected the most informative article and proceeded to summarize my key findings. After analyzing the information provided in the article I wrapped things up with my opinion on how we can go about preventing stress as a society. This project is a good display of my investigative journalism skills since I swiftly browsed multiple resources, spotted common trends and analyzed them, and concluded with my advice.

Spring 2019

Project Title: Unconditional Cash Transfers: The Future of Aid (Global Development INTL-I203, Spring 2019)

Group Members: None

Project Description: The goal of this essay was to present my opinion on the most effecient method to provide economic aid to underdeveloped countries. I started by listing off the various global aid programs we had learned about over the course of the semester, and factually proved why each program was inefficient. Next, I stated why my chosen program was the most economically efficient method to go about things by providing precedent of successful implementations in various countries. This paper is a great display of my global awareness and ability to simplify and analyze extremely complex international issues.
