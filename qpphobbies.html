Astronomy

I've always enjoyed looking at stars in the clear night sky. In 2021 I decided to take an introductory astronomy course. This course was extremely interesting and provoked me to begin thinking about the universe in a whole different persepctive. I started attending the general public Kirkwood Observatory Nights (pictured in slide #2) which are run by IU astronomy PHD students. I will never forget the first time I saw Jupiter and Saturn through that telescope, it was an indescribable experience. Since then I have been heavily invested in expanded my knowledge of space via theoretical data analysis with python, and many nights out observing the cosmic wonderland.
